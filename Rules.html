PhishAID Rule Framework (v1.0)

Implemented in Version 1.0

  • Rules 1–10 — URL & Transport-level analysis
  • Rule 18 — Clone phishing (DOM similarity heuristic)
  • Rules 21–22 — Identity deception (homoglyph & typosquatting)
  • Rule 30 — Semantic phishing intent (heuristic-based)

Reserved for Future Work

Rules 11–17, 19, 20, 23, 25, 27–29 are part of the PhishAID design framework but are not implemented in v1.0 due to higher engineering, crawling, or dataset requirements.
