About PhishAID

PhishAID is an academic cybersecurity project focused on detecting phishing websites using a transparent and explainable rule-based engine.

The project is developed as part of an academic curriculum and aligns with national-level cybersecurity and AI challenge problem statements.

Unlike black-box machine learning models, PhishAID emphasizes interpretability, explainability, and rule transparency, making it suitable for education and research.
