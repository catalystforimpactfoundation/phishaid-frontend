About PhishAID

PhishAID is an academic cybersecurity project focused on detecting phishing websites using a transparent and explainable rule-based engine.

The project was developed as part of an academic curriculum and emphasizes infrastructure awareness, security principles, and research alignment.

Unlike black-box machine learning systems, PhishAID prioritizes interpretability, making it suitable for education, research, and demonstration purposes.
